# Pokémon Of The Day - Patch notes
  
  ## v1.2.0 - Changés X and Y

* Profile has been added - you can access it from Pokédex START button and it contains statistics about your total Pokémon count, shinies, most common type of Pokémon you caught as well as their region
* Brand new inventory system has been added: You can now obtain an **Odd Egg** granted every **7th** Pokémon you caught (backwards compatibility in place - you will be granted respective amount of eggs based on how much Pokémons you caught so far)
* Odd Egg can be hatched for **24h**, one at a time. After that time you can revisit your inventory to finish hatching process and be granted instantly a bonus Pokémon with  **shiny odds 1 / 7** (feature from Pokémon Crystal)
* There are no reminders or notifications set in place for hatching apart from the status in your inventory - this is to respect your attention and time. The bot is made for *you* when *you* want and can - not the other way around
* Reset timer has been adjusted from **12h** to **Midnight** Central European Time UTC (you can check your cooldown with `potd` command)
